Sign Language to Text Converter

The "Sign Language to Text Converter" project aims to bridge communication gaps by translating sign language gestures into readable text in real-time. Leveraging computer vision and machine learning technologies, this innovative system recognizes and interprets sign language movements, converting them into corresponding text output. By providing a seamless and accessible means of communication, this project strives to enhance inclusivity and facilitate easier interactions between individuals using sign language and those who rely on written communication.

Space dash

"Space Dash" is a delightful casual game crafted within a single day using Godot. Players navigate a charming astronaut who leaps between colorful planets, aiming for the highest score by strategically timing jumps to land on each celestial body. With simple controls and vibrant visuals, this quick and engaging game promises a thrilling space-hopping experience that challenges reflexes and offers endless fun.
